About Us

Aadhar Samajik Sanstha is a NGO across Pune

Aadhaar Samajik Sanstha is a Non-Governmental Organization registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 and Bombay Public Trust Act 1950.


What do we do

Aadhaar Baal Aangan is a project of Aadhaar Samajik Sanstha that lays down a strong foundation for the development of children of Construction Labourers from the age group of 1 month to 16 years through ensuring education, nutrition, health services, recreation, protection and community participation at construction sites.

Our Vision

The major focus of this organization is to create awareness among the marginalized about importance of education, personal/community health and hygiene and conserving environment.

Our Mission

A strong foundation for the development of children of Construction Laborers from the age group of 1 month to 16 years through ensuring education, nutrition, health services, recreation, protection, and community participation at construction sites.
