How can you volunteer

Become a part of Aadhaar Samajik Sanstha and contribute to the noble cause

Donate

One can support monetarily for the development of the children of construction labourers. The support received from you will surely lift the position of our organization and which on the other hand, help us take our quality interventions to its height. Donations to Aadhaar Samajik Sanstha are exempted under Section 80 G of The Income Tax Act 1961.

Mid Day Meals

"No one, nowhere, at no time, should go hungry."
Donations in terms of grocery will help us to provide nutrition to the children of construction labourers.

Collection Drives

One can donate in-kind through collection drives. Any material, new or used, such as story books, educational games, toys, sports material, utensils, clothes, footwear, etc. can be donated. These will be further used by the children of construction labourers.

Volunteer

One can visit our activity centres and conduct an activity or a session based on language, math, general science (solar system, environment, experiments, etc.), art and craft (origami, paper bags), teaching-learning aids, national and social reformers, community helpers, sports, music and dance.

We are nonprofit team
and work across Pune

Aadhaar Samajik Sanstha is a Non-Governmental Organization registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 and Bombay Public Trust Act 1950.

The major focus of the organization is to create awareness among the marginalized about importance of education, personal/community health and hygiene and conserving environment.

Major causes

We broadly focus on these causes for children at construction sites

Education

Education is a basic human right. Children of construction workers are deprived from their right to education. Hence, we ensure that every child is educated and is comfortable to face the world later.

Nutrition

Children of construction labourers are unable to obtain a balanced diet with right nutrition. This affects their health and concentration. Hence, we ensure daily nutritious mid-day meals to the children which increases their health standards and makes them active.

Some of the activities

Some events that we keep conducting

Communication and Confidence building of children

Sessions held in which every children is encouraged to lead and teach other students to boost self confidence and value self learning.

Educational trips

Educational trips conducted to various places such as Zoology Park, Science Park, 3D educational show, Exhibitions, etc. to promote practical learning.

Vaccination

On campus vaccination given by medical professionals to all the children so that they can live a healthy and disease free life.

Yoga

Yoga sessions conducted regularly to teach the importance of fitness to children.
