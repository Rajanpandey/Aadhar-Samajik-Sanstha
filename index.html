Aadhaar Samajik Sanstha is a Non-Governmental Organization registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 and Bombay Public Trust Act 1950.

The major focus of the organization is to create awareness among the marginalized about importance of education, personal/community health and hygiene and conserving environment.

Major causes

We broadly focus on these causes for children at construction sites

Ensuring education

Education is a basic human right for every children. Children of construction workers don't always get the right education so we ensure that every child is educated properly and is comfortable to face the world later.

Ensuring nutrition

Many children are unable to obtain a balanced diet with right nutritions. This leads to the feeling of unmotivated and often feeling sick. We provide mid-day meals to the children with a balanced diet so that they stay healthy and motivated.

Community participation

We enroll the students into the nearest government school after covering their pre-primary education. They are often shy and dont open up easily. So we conduct various activities to get involved with people to boost their confidence.

Our Vision

Our Mission

A strong foundation for the development of children of Construction Laborers from the age group of 1 month to 16 years through ensuring education, nutrition, health services, recreation, protection, and community participation at construction sites.
