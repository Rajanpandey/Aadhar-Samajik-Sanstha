Our Vision

The major focus of this organization is to create awareness among the marginalized about importance of education, personal/community health and hygiene and conserving environment.

Our Mission

A strong foundation for the development of children of Construction Laborers from the age group of 1 month to 16 years through ensuring education, nutrition, health services, recreation, protection, and community participation at construction sites.
